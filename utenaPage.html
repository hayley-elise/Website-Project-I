When her parents died, Utena Tenjou was helped out of her depression by a handsome prince; he gave her a ring to let her remember the occasion. She was so impressed by him, she vowed to become a prince herself one day!
Now in middle school, she finds herself drawn into a series of duels to win the hand of Anthy Himemiya, the Rose Bride who possesses the power to "revolutionise the world."
As a side note, this show is riddled with hidden meanings. I still have trouble understanding some things myself!

Utena is on the right, Anthy on the left.
As the Rose Bride, Anthy follows the orders of whoever is engaged to her. She is extremely submissive, but very sweet.
ChuChu is one of Anthy's many pets, but the most adorable by far.

Utena is known around school for her tomboyish ways... and her strong will to wear a boy's uniform.
